MARS ROVERS







SOJOURNER






Sojourner is the Mars Pathfinder robotic Mars rover that landed on mars in the Ares Vallis region, and explored Mars for around three months.







OPPORTUNITY and SPIRIT










NASA's Mars Exploration Rover mission was a robotic space mission involving two Mars rovers, Spirit and Opportunity, exploring the planet Mars.







CURIOCITY





Curiosity is a car-sized rover designed to explore the crater Gale on Mars as part of NASA's Mars Science Laboratory mission.
